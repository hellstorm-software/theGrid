Welcome to the world of Sxlaris

Kult Rising is a sandbox RPG set in the Sxlaris universe, an alternate reality where anomalous SCPs roam freely and megacorporations control the world's governing powers. Dive into a dark sci-fi/fantasy world filled with dungeons to crawl, backrooms levels and Foundation sites waiting to be explored, and a survival crafting systemto tie it all together.



Live Service Content

The game will be recieving continuous updates for the foreseeable future. This includes new story quests, new game modes, and new side quests. Kult Rising is the main vehicle of delivery for the Sxlaris Universe as a worldbuilding project and as such, will grow continuously as the Universe itself does. This is one of the core design philosophies of the game, with our goal being to create an accurate representation of what we imagine Sxlaris to be.

Currently Implemented Features:

Idle Game

You appear to be stuck somwhere on Floor 7, near the elevator. A strange robotic creature nearby appears to be connected to a control panel. Use the control panel to direct this drone to collect spice or resources. Spice serves as a fuel source for constructing additional drones, while resources can be stockpiled for building and crafting.



p


Planned Features:

Global Market

Kult Rising will feature a Global Exchange, where players can list, buy, and sell, items to other players with Gold, the main currency of the Sxlaris Universe. This means the world will feature a dynamic market that fluctuates with the supply and the demand of the players.



Connected Game System

The Sxlaris universe of games (currently consisting of Kult Rising and PyxieBot) all share a connected inventory and character system. This means playing any one of our games benefits your accounts in the others. This also means that you can start grinding for free through our discord bot - Pyxie - before ever purchasing one of our games.
