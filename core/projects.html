HellStorm Software

HellStorm Software is the official game/software development studio of Sxlar Wasteland. We have 2 main games at the moment, Genestealer and Biohack, along with our discord bot Pyxie.


p

Genestealer is a 2D experimental RPG, with dozens of game modes/systems, all of which allow you to improve your character, from your standard fighting game, to an incremental clicker game, a roguelike dungeon, and a captivating story.


p

Biohack is a text based Hacking Simulator, where you play as a pen-tester in the Sxlaris Universe, and search to find the secret hidden in the machine. This game is a multi-user dungeon, or MUD, meaning that what you create, change, and interact with will be seen by every other player who visits that sector.



Sxlar Stock

Sxlar Stock is the official public asset library of Sxlar Wasteland. With a huge collection of images, videos, sound effects, music, overlays, and any digital asset you could need, Sxlar Stock is the number one asset library for creatives.

We have a free library for anyone to use, and a patreon where you can find even more assets for just $5 a month.



House Atreides

House Atreides is an independent music label, and the official music label/publisher of Sxlar Wasteland. While we primarily focus on experimental, hip hop, and edm styles of music, our artists specialize in versatility and have produced music across dozens of genres.



DONT TRUST GOD

DONT TRUST GOD is the official clothing line of Sxlar Wasteland, specializing in techwear, streetwear, and darkwear. Taking themes from cyberpunk, sci-fi, military, and dystopian fashion, we live by out motto: Just Be Weird

Sxlar Closet is a zero-waste fashion project that takes old clothes and other pieces of fabric, and refurbishes/upcycles them into brand new pieces.
