Welcome to the very first "Offline MMO"

Now that term is an oxymoron, so let us explain. Genestealer is an expermintal 2D RPG, that is designed to feel like an MMO, but not require a full server.

This means we can run a continuously updating, large scale game, without charging any subscriptions or needing to worry about server uptimes. Now the social and online elements of MMO's are one of their core pillars, so in order to make up for this defecit, we have implemented a few systems.



Live Service Content

The game will be recieving continuous updates for the foreseeable future (with the only plan to end support being after the launch of the 3D version of Genestealer). This includes new story quests, new game modes, and new side quests. Genestealer is the main vehicle of delivery for the Sxlaris Universe as a worldbuilding project and as such, will grow continuously as the Universe itself does. This is one of the core design philosophies of the game, with our goal being to create an accurate representation of what we imagine Sxlaris to be.

Planned Features:

Global Market

Genestealer features a Global Exchange, where players can list, buy, and sell, items to other players with Gold, the main currency of the Sxlaris Universe. This means the world will feature a dynamic market that fluctuates with the supply and the demand of the players.



Hoarde Mechanic

Genestealer allows for the creation of up to 10 characters, with all of them having a shared bank of items, gear, and equipment. This is an in universe mechanic that allows you to play through the differnet paths available in the story. Choices matter here. What you say and do to NPCs will have dramatic outcomes on the world and characters around you. The Hoarde feature allows you to freely explore the world without having to fear messing up their world in a permanent way.



Connected Game System

The Sxlaris universe of games (currently consisting of biohack, Genestealer, and PyxieBot) all share a connected inventory and character system. This means playing any one of our games benefits your accounts in the others. This also means that you can start grinding for free through our discord bot - Pyxie - before ever purchasing one of our games.
