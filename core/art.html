Rinzlxrd's Everydays:

After 2 and a half years, the "Everydays" project has been postponed indefinitely so I have more time to focus on the other aspects of Sxlar Wasteland.
(Although someone else may be picking up the mantle in the near future).

These pictures are all done from start to finish every day in a single day. The purpose of this project is to help me get better at different aspects
of my art by forcing myself to practice every day. I am currently on my 2nd chapter of everydays. This year I'll be creating a piece everyday using
Photoshop. Because I am rather familiar with PS, I will be focusing on honing and developing my specific art style.
